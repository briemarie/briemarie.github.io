ABOUT.ME.BRIE.MARIE

I am a published poet turned creative coder, an art enthusiast and painter transforming personal aesthetic appeals into front-end web and mobile app design. I believe in inventing and reinventing myself often. When I was eleven, I trained myself to be left handed. I am a right-brain, big-picture thinker balancing the world of linear logic and reason. I am impassioned by efforts to reduce human trafficking, both locally and globally. I believe in dancing and laughing as much as possible. I love foggy mornings and espresso, making San Francisco and Seattle my two favorite cities.

Dev Bootcamp

I graduated from Dev Bootcamp in San Francisco in January of 2015. I completed over 1000 hours of coding in the 18 week program, and feel confident and prepared to start work as a full-stack web and mobile app developer. Areas of focus included Ruby, server and client side Rails, Active Record, functional and OO JS, HTML5 and CSS3 design, single page apps with AJAX and jQuery, front-end AngularJS, and iOS mobile builds with Ionic.

University of California, Berkeley

I graduated with my BA in English from UC Berkeley in May of 2014. The emphasis of my studies was romantic and modern poetry, and I was awarded a spot in the Phi Beta Kappa Honor Society. While attending, I had four poems published in various literary magazines. I also helped found an all women's meditation group on campus, Fiat Lux for Women.

Mira Costa Community College

I graduated from Mira Costa Community College in Oceanside, California with my AA in Social and Behavioral Sciences in May of 2012. The emphasis of my studies was in various areas of psychology, and I was awarded a spot in the Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society. I had my first poem published in a local literary magazine, and volunteered many hours helping with community activities sponsored by the astronomy department.
