ABOUT me

i am a published poet turned creative coder, translating my knowledge of the symbolic language of poetry into the symbolic language of computer programs. i am fascinated by the overlap between writing literature and writing code, machine learning and the future of Ai, the mind and consciousness, astronomy and physics, and cultivating the humanness that drives successful teams and businesses. i have worked professionally as a software engineer and as an engineering team lead in the US and Australia. i believe in dancing daily and laughing hourly, at least. The following musings are my attempt at bridging the diverse thoughts that makes me excited to wake up every morning, no matter where in the world i may find myself.
